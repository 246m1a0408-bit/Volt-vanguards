Lead Scoring

Lead Scoring uses Artificial Intelligence and machine learning to analyze customer behavior, engagement history, and demographic data to rank leads based on their likelihood to convert.

How It Works:

  • Analyzes website visits and interactions
  • Tracks email engagement
  • Evaluates past buying behavior
  • Assigns conversion probability score

Benefits:

  • Focus on high-quality leads
  • Improve sales efficiency
  • Increase conversion rates
